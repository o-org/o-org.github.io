Contribute

Support Our Digital Projects and Help Special Kids

We are a different kind of social startup. We do not accept donations. Thanks to that, we do not rely on donations. Instead of donations, we are developing and providing technology solutions and generating income from those projects.

But you can help kids with special needs via donating for them. We have a gift card system; you can buy Otsimo's product for special kids.

email us: [email protected]

How Does It Work?

When you buy a premium to a kid, we are donating Otsimo Premium to our parent organizations. After that, they are providing Otsimo for free to families in the most need.
