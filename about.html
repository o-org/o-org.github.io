    Activities

    Our programs are developed to make sure that support goes directly to the families dealing with the everyday challenges and financial difficulties that come with raising a special child.

    Special Education ABA

    Our Special Education program has more than 100 games in one app. It is democratizing access to special education through its leading game-based learning app, teaching children core skills vital to cognitive and behavioral development.

    Speech Therapy SLP

    Our Speech Therapy program is a voice-controlled and video modeling-based language and speech therapy app. Individuals with speech delays, stuttering, apraxia, non-verbal autism, and late talkers can easily use the app for their language and speech development.

    Supporting Contents for Families

    We are providing special education, speech therapy, ABA therapy and IEP content for free to families. Thanks to this project, families can reach more than 100+ articles about special conditions and special education.
